About Us



Last updated: 06-18-2023


Welcome to Nnet, your neighborhood social media app!


  1. Goal

    1. At Nnet, we believe that strong communities are built on connections, and our platform aims to bring people together in their local neighborhoods. Our mission is to create a digital space where neighbors can connect, share information, and build meaningful relationships.
    2. Whether you're looking for recommendations for local businesses, organizing community events, or simply wanting to engage with your neighbors, Nnet provides the perfect platform to do so. Our user-friendly interface and intuitive features make it easy to connect with your neighborhood and stay informed about what's happening around you.

  2. Key features of Nnet:

    1. Neighborhood-specific newsfeed: Stay up-to-date with posts, announcements, and events from your local community.
    2. Local business recommendations: Discover and share recommendations for restaurants, shops, services, and more in your neighborhood.
    3. Community groups: Join or create groups based on shared interests, hobbies, or local initiatives.
    4. Private messaging: Connect with your neighbors through private messages for more personal interactions.
    5. Events calendar: Explore and RSVP to community events happening in your neighborhood.

  3. Key features of Nnet:

    1. At Nnet, we prioritize privacy and data security. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe and trusted environment for all users.
    2. Join Nnet today and start connecting with your neighbors and building a stronger community!
